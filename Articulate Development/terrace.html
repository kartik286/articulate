Terrace Gardening

Terrace gardening allows you to utilize unused rooftop or terrace space effectively.
Terrace gardens can provide an opportunity to grow fresh produce or create a green space
Plants help filter and purify the air by absorbing carbon dioxide and releasing oxygen through photosynthesis.
Terrace gardens can enhance the visual appeal of your home or building.
They add greenery and natural beauty to the surroundings, creating a pleasant and soothing environment.
The presence of plants on the terrace can provide additional insulation, reducing heat transfer into the building.
This can contribute to improved energy efficiency by reducing the need for air conditioning during hot weather.

Advantages

  • Efficient Use of Space
  • Improved Air Quality
  • Access to Fresh and Organic Produce
  • Improved Insulation and Energy Efficiency

Disadvantages

  • Structural Load and Waterproofing
  • Maintenance and Irrigation
  • Exposure to Weather Conditions
  • Limited Space,Plant Selection,Accessibility and Safety
