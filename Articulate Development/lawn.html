Lawn Maintenance

A lush green lawn can create a welcoming and inviting atmosphere.
awns provide a space for outdoor activities, such as playing sports, hosting gatherings, or simply relaxing.
They offer a soft surface for children to play on and can be a great space for socializing with friends and family.
Lawns can help control soil erosion by stabilizing the soil with their root systems.
Grass absorbs heat from the sun and releases moisture through evaporation, creating a cooling effect.
Lawns can contribute to a healthier environment by absorbing carbon dioxide, a greenhouse gas that contributes to climate change.
Lawns can also filter and purify rainwater, reducing runoff and helping to replenish groundwater

Advantages

  • Erosion Control
  • Environmental Benefits
  • Cooling Effect
  • Recreational Use

Disadvantages

  • Lawn requires regular care, including mowing, watering, fertilizing, and weed control.
  • Lawn maintenance practices involve the use of chemicals
  • Maintaining a lawn can be expensive
  • Ecological Impact
